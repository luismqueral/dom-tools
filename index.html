What is this?

A tiny floating toolbar for describing interface changes to LLMs.

Point at any element, write what you'd change in a popover, edit text inline, then hit copy to send all your changes to your model of choice. No Tailwind dependency.

How does it work?

Hover Move your cursor around. Elements outline pink so you can see their boundaries.
Click Selects the element and opens a small popover. Type the change you want.
Edit text Click an already-selected text element again to drop the caret in. Edits are tracked alongside your notes.
Esc / blur Closes the popover. Your note stays visible as a pink bubble anchored to the element.
Annotate Free-hand draw mode for sketching directly on the page. Toggle from the toolbar or hit A.
Full page screenshot Capture the entire scrollable page as PNG. Cmd+Shift+S or click the camera button.

What are the shortcuts?

Cmd+Shift+K / Ctrl+Shift+K toggle inspector

Esc Esc (double-tap) re-focus the cursor tool

Cmd+Shift+S / Ctrl+Shift+S full page screenshot

Esc exit the current popover or tool mode

Camera and Annotate modes are toggled via the toolbar buttons.
