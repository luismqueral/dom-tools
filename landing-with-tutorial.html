What is this?

A tiny floating toolbar for describing interface changes — not making them. You point at elements, write what should change, and DOM-Tools packages those instructions as structured Markdown. Paste it into an LLM (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.) and the model applies the changes to your code.

Think of it as a design brief generator: you articulate the intent, your LLM executes it.
